Delete Account Request

Game: Roll Bounce
Developer: Von Base Enterprises

Request Account & Data Deletion

To request deletion of your Roll Bounce account and associated data, please send an email to:

support@vonbase.com

Please include the following in your request:

  • Your User ID or email address associated with your account
  • Confirmation that you want your account and data deleted

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete:

  • Your account information (User ID, email if provided)
  • Leaderboard entries and scores
  • Game progress and statistics
  • Any other personal data associated with your account
Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your leaderboard rankings and game progress will be lost.

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Aggregated Data: Some anonymized, aggregated data (such as total player counts or average scores) may be retained for analytics purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify you.

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us at support@vonbase.com.
